Privacy Policy for ChromaViewer

Last updated: December 22, 2024

Overview

ChromaViewer is a chromatogram file viewer for AB1 and SCF format files. This app is provided by Wildtype Technologies, LLC and is designed with your privacy in mind.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

All file processing happens entirely on your device. When you open a chromatogram file (.ab1 or .scf):

  • The file is processed locally on your device
  • No data is uploaded to any server
  • No data is shared with Wildtype Technologies, LLC or any third parties
  • No analytics or tracking services are used

Permissions

ChromaViewer requires the following permission:

  • File Access: To allow you to select and open chromatogram files from your device

This permission is used solely for the purpose of letting you choose which files to view in the app.

Third-Party Services

The app includes a BLAST search feature that opens the NCBI BLAST website in your device's web browser. When you use this feature, you are subject to NCBI's privacy policy and terms of service.

Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, your files remain completely private and secure on your device.

Children's Privacy

ChromaViewer does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
